Horimiya

The story is about two high school students, Hori who is quite popular in school and Miyamura who has a nerd-like appearance.
However, they aren't who they seem outside of school. They both help each other to hide their identities outside of school,
but this eventually leads to something more.
I chose Horimiya because I found it wholesome and something to relate to.
